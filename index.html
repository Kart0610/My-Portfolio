About Me

Get to know me better

I am a Full Stack Java Developer with a deliberate transition from Chemistry to software development. I completed my B.Sc in Chemistry from SDNB Vaishnav College for Women, Chennai in 2024. After graduation, driven by a passion for building web applications and solving real-world problems, I transitioned into technology and completed the Full Stack Java Development course at GUVI (IIT-Madras incubated EdTech platform) in 2025. My chemistry background sharpened my analytical thinking and problem-solving skills—qualities I now apply to designing robust, scalable web applications.

During the course, I gained hands-on experience in Java, Spring Boot, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Tailwind CSS, MySQL, PostgreSQL, Git, and REST APIs. I built real-world projects including a Movie Ticket Booking System, Real Estate Listing Platform, and Smart-Spend Expense Tracker—each demonstrating my ability to deliver end-to-end solutions from concept to deployment.

I am passionate about building web applications that make a difference. My structured, analytical approach helps me break down complex requirements and write clean, maintainable code. I am confident in my ability to contribute to a team, learn quickly, and deliver quality software. I am eager to bring my technical skills and problem-solving mindset to a growth-oriented organization.
